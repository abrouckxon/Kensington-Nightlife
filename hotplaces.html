    • SuperMarket

    268 Agusta Ave. (5pm-1am)

    Cover? Yes.


    SuperMarket has been and still is a lively dancehall/ bar combination. With Friday and Saturdays DJ's playing the hits. With the length of the bar having seating on the oppsoite side against the wall in the forms of tables and booths. The back 1/3rd of the establishment having a dancefloor with minimal seating in that area. the vibe of Supermarket can only be described as "An extroverts dream" where in if most people are there just to get drinks they can quickly be convinced to join on the dancefloor it just needs the first few people to break the mold and get everyone on the floor. Personally I have had many experiences where the dancefloor is completley empty but within 5 minutes of someone starting to dance the entire place migrates to the dancefloor. With reasonbly priced drinks and relatively friendly staff SuperMarket is definitley worth a chance!

    -Ramp - All one level - Security - Open Floor - In & Out - Patio (summer only) - Wheelchair accessable





    HandleBar

    223 Agusta Ave. (3pm-2am)

    Cover? No

    Handlebar established in 2012 is newest addition to the kensington scene. A fun bar with always something going on, from their recuring "Hot Breath Karaoke" to their Wild Wild Party, and drag shows. Handlebar truly has something for everyone. Located at the end of Kensington Market, great for smaller groups due to the dispersed seating although with prior arrangments. Handlebar also wants it's patrons aware that they are very inclusive to anyone and everyone proudly displaying flags in support of Queer and Trans friends and customers. So if you're looking for a palce with something always going on, checkout Handlebar!

    - In & Out - Cash Only - Patio - Stairs to Bathroom - No Wheelchair access

    - In & Out - Wheelchair Accesable - Patio - Stairs to Bathroom
